Plan of our adventures

First stop - fortress

During this amazing adventure you will be able to get to know Biokovo mountain all the way to very top the third largest mountain peak in Croatia. Experience the beauty and diversity if the Biokovo Nature Park with a panoramic ride. The seemingly clumsy and rugged mountain has an abundance of animal and plant species,of which up to 60 Biokovo endemic species. The castle in Kotišina is the best preserved fortification in Makarska. Up to now on Biokovo over 400 caves have been found.

Second stop - top of biokovo

Biokovo is the largest, most beautiful and longest mountain in Dalmatia and every visit offers a special experience. From the top of the hill at an altitude of 1762 m, they are unmistakable photo motifs for true respect of pure nature. When the weather is very clear, from the top of Biokovo it is possible to see Monte Gargano in Italy, which is 252 km (157 mi) away. Church of St.Jure on Biokovo is the higest church in Croatia. Biokovo was named for its snowy whiteness.

Third stop - lunch

With brandy made of 48 healing herbs and a delicious lunch,you will relax in the traditionally decorated ambience and enjoy a free-moving animal ranch. Climbing or desceding on Biokovo it is worth to stop there and take a look over Makarska. Meat is traditional and fine. We will enjoy a walk around farm and see numerous farm animals like horses,cows,donkeys. The view is amazing.
